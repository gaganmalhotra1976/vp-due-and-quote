Change Password

To change the app password, open browser console (F12) and run:

crypto.subtle.digest('SHA-256', new TextEncoder().encode('YourNewPassword')).then(b=>console.log([...new Uint8Array(b)].map(x=>x.toString(16).padStart(2,'0')).join('')))

Copy the hash, then update STAFF_HASH at the top of the JS in index.html.
